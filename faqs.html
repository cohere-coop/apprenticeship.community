Frequently Asked Questions

Who Should Join?

We welcome anyone who is interested in building cultures of learning and growth at work, no matter their industry. Our members range from technology executives to artists. We value skills of all kinds and all levels.

Who Are You?

We’re people with a dream of a world where cultures of growth and learning at work are the norm, not the exception. The current “staff” is Zee Spencer, a software engineer, and Kiera Manion-Fischer, an editor and writer. The Apprenticeship Community is a project of Zinc.

Where Does The Money Go?

We use the money to pay authors and event special guests, our editor, and our community caretaker. We donate 10% of our revenue (after processing fees) to partner organizations that provide sustaining, educating, and actualizing services to under-priviliged or under-represented groups.
