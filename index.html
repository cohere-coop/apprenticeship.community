The Apprenticeship Community is a safe-to-learn environment full of interesting and talented people who recognize that the best way to improve is to be open about what they don't know and get advice from others. While we do charge a nominal $5 fee to join, those who require assistance may waive the fee by sending an email to hello-ac@zincma.de. We strive to be a respectful, inclusive community and have a strong code of conduct.

If this sounds like something you're interested in, fill out our application form to become a member today! For those of you who are already members, log in to our chat!
