We're an online publication and community of cross-skilled practitioners fostering work cultures of learning and growth around the world. We provide:

  • An accessible, friendly, peer-learning focused chat room
  • Compelling articles about principles, practices and experiences pertinent to actively-learning professionals.
  • Scheduled community ask-me-anything's with practitioners and leaders, giving community members a chance to learn from experts.
  • Regular Twitter chats on #Apprenticeship
  • Scheduled community topic explorations, where we dive into a specific topic relevant to professional learners.
